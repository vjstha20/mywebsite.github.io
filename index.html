More About Me.

I specialize in Brand Development & Identity Design. Helping ambitious & adventurous businesses build bold brands that stand out from the crowd by effectively connecting them with their audiences.

Ever since I was a kid, software development has been my passion. I still remember sitting in front of a computer and learning QBasic. Being able to get the computer to do whatever I wanted, was almost like a magic for me. This passion carried me to get enrolled in the Bachelor of Sciences in Computer Science and Information Technology (Bsc.CSIT) program provided by Tribhuvan University, one of the oldest and prestigious universities in Nepal. And it was here that I learned truly how much there was yet to learn.

I have seen many brands struggling for their identity in the crowd. This made me inclined towards Business and soon I realized the importance of Digital Marketing and Brand Development. Then I decided to help ambitious & adventurous businesses build bold brands that stand out from the crowd by effectively connecting them with their audiences. Now I freelance as a digital marketing agent and provide various brand development services to my clients.
